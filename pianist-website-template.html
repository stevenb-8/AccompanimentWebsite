About Me

Professional Multi-Instrumentalist with Over 20 Years of Experience

Welcome to my website! I am a dedicated accompanist, coach, and musical director based in Sacramento, CA. With a passion for music that began at an early age, I have developed a versatile repertoire spanning pop, jazz, and contemporary styles played primarily on guitar or piano. I have a uniquely gifted ear and would love to accompany your next show or event.

Since 2022, I have served as a musical director and accompanist for shows ranging from staged musicals to live underscoring and cocktail hours. I specialize in improvisation and can make any evening a night of many musical delights!

As a professional accompanist, I pride myself on my ability to create unforgettable arrangements and bring rich new flavors to popular tunes, adapting my style to complement actors, instrumentalists, and vocalists across various genres and skill levels.

Whether you need an accompanist for a show or special event, I am committed to delivering a polished and professional performance tailored to your specific needs.
